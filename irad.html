Why Aerospace & Defense Cybersecurity

Modern defense platforms are increasingly software-defined and network-connected — yet cybersecurity processes for these systems lag years behind enterprise IT. Avionics engineers are experts in safety, not security. Embedded developers optimize for determinism, not threat modeling. The regulatory frameworks exist (DO-326A, MIL-STD-882, RMF), but tooling to implement them efficiently does not.

This IRAD track fills that gap — building tools that speak the language of aerospace engineers: airworthiness evidence, safety case arguments, deterministic RTOS constraints, and certification schedules — while delivering the rigor of modern cybersecurity practice.
