Active Research Initiatives

Six concurrent IRAD programs spanning technology readiness levels 3–6, targeting near-term transition to DoD contractor environments.

// PRJ-001 — CASSETTE
Active Development
CMMC AutoScore Engine
AI-powered CMMC Level 2 practice assessment engine. Ingests scan data, configuration baselines, and artifact evidence to auto-score practices, generate pre-populated SSP narratives, and produce a prioritized POA&M with estimated remediation effort.
TRL Indicator TRL 4 — Lab Validated
Python NLP CMMC L2 NIST 800-171 ML Scoring
// PRJ-002 — MERIDIAN
Prototype
CUI Cartographer
Automated Controlled Unclassified Information data flow discovery and boundary visualization. Combines passive network fingerprinting with a classification ML model to map CUI across systems, storage, and communication channels — producing live boundary diagrams audit-ready for C3PAO review.
TRL Indicator TRL 3 — Proof of Concept
Graph Theory Network Scan Zero Trust Data Class. D3.js
// PRJ-003 — WATCHTOWER
Active Development
SENTINEL-ZT Framework
Zero Trust Architecture reference implementation and maturity assessment framework purpose-built for defense contractors. Aligns to NIST SP 800-207 and DoD ZT Strategy, providing a scored roadmap from legacy perimeter-based security to full zero trust in five measurable phases.
TRL Indicator TRL 5 — Relevant Environment
Zero Trust NIST 800-207 DoD ZT Strategy Assessment
// PRJ-004 — VIPER
Active Development
ThreatMatrix Intelligence
Adversarial threat intelligence aggregation platform with ML-powered TTP extraction and auto-mapping to MITRE ATT&CK for ICS and Enterprise. Ingests open-source threat feeds, ISAC advisories, and CISA KEV to produce actor-tagged risk scores aligned to active CMMC controls.
TRL Indicator TRL 4 — Lab Validated
MITRE ATT&CK Threat Intel NLP CISA KEV Risk Scoring
// PRJ-005 — ANVIL
Advanced Prototype
SSP Forge
Automated System Security Plan generation engine that produces structured, DoD-compliant SSP documents from control inventory scans and policy repositories. Uses large language model fine-tuning to generate implementation narratives, preserving technical accuracy while dramatically reducing authoring time from months to days.
TRL Indicator TRL 6 — Demonstrated
LLM Fine-Tuning SSP DFARS Python FastAPI
// PRJ-006 — LIGHTHOUSE
Research Phase
C-SCRM Radar
Supply chain risk intelligence platform for defense contractors. Builds a vendor dependency graph, auto-scores third-party risk against NIST SP 800-161 controls, and flags critical single-source components. Designed to support CMMC SR domain compliance and DFARS supply chain security requirements.
TRL Indicator TRL 3 — Proof of Concept
C-SCRM NIST 800-161 Graph Analysis Risk Scoring

TRL Reference

Technology Readiness Levels (TRL) per DoD 5000.02 — standard metric for measuring maturity of a technology from basic research through operational deployment.

TRL 1–2 — Basic research & concept
TRL 3 — Proof of concept
TRL 4 — Lab validated
TRL 5 — Relevant environment
TRL 6 — Demonstrated
TRL 7–8 — System prototype / qualified
TRL 9 — Operational system proven
Current portfolio range: TRL 3 – 6
