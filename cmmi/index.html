CMMI Institute Version 2.0

Maturity Levels

Maturity levels represent defined evolutionary plateaus for process improvement. An organization at a given level has satisfied all goals of the practice areas at that level and all lower levels.

1
Initial

Processes are unpredictable, poorly controlled, and reactive. Success depends on individual heroics rather than repeatable process.

ML1
2
Managed

Projects are planned, monitored, and controlled. Requirements are managed; processes are established and followed on individual projects.

ML2
3
Defined

Processes are well-characterized, standardized, and drawn from the organization's process library. Proactive rather than reactive improvement.

ML3
4
Quantitatively Managed

Quantitative objectives for quality and performance are established. Statistical methods are used to understand and control process variation.

ML4
5
Optimizing

Continuous process improvement is enabled through quantitative feedback and from piloting innovative ideas and technologies.

Practice Areas

Showing 27 practice areas

EST Core ML2 ML3

Estimating

Establish and maintain estimates of the size, effort, duration, and cost of work, and provide a rationale for those estimates.

PLAN Core ML2 ML3

Planning

Establish and maintain plans that define the work to be performed, the resources required, and the approach to delivering value.

MC Core ML2 ML3

Monitor and Control

Track actual performance against plans, identify deviations, and take corrective action to achieve planned performance.

GOV Core ML2 ML3

Governance

Ensure that executive-level sponsorship, sponsorship direction, and organizational authority are in place to sustain process improvement.

II Core ML2 ML3

Implementation Infrastructure

Establish and maintain the infrastructure needed to plan and implement the process improvement effort.

CM Core ML2 ML3

Configuration Management

Establish and maintain the integrity of identified work products using configuration identification, control, status accounting, and audits.

PQA Core ML2 ML3

Process Quality Assurance

Objectively evaluate processes and work products against applicable process descriptions, standards, and procedures.

WM Core ML2 ML3

Workforce Management

Identify, develop, and retain the knowledge, skills, and abilities the organization needs to accomplish its work.

MPM Core ML2 ML3 ML4

Managing Performance and Measurement

Establish and maintain the measurement infrastructure to support management information needs across the organization.

RSK Core ML2 ML3

Risk and Opportunity Management

Identify and manage risks and opportunities to proactively reduce the likelihood of negative impacts and increase the likelihood of positive ones.

RDM Core ML2 ML3

Requirements Development and Management

Develop and manage customer, product, and product-component requirements and ensure alignment between those requirements and plans and work products.

SAM Core ML2 ML3

Supplier Agreement Management

Manage the acquisition of products and services from suppliers to satisfy requirements while managing risk throughout the agreement.

PR Core ML3

Peer Reviews

Systematically identify and remove defects early using structured peer reviews, inspections, and walkthroughs of work products.

OT Core ML3

Organizational Training

Develop the skills and knowledge to ensure people can perform their roles effectively and support organizational performance.

DAR Core ML3

Decision Analysis and Resolution

Analyze possible decisions using a formal evaluation process that identifies alternatives against established criteria.

PAD Core ML3

Process Asset Development

Establish and maintain the organization's process library — the set of standard processes, guidelines, and tools available to projects.

PCM Core ML3 ML4 ML5

Process Change Management

Continually improve process capability by identifying process improvements and deploying them across the organization.

CAR Core ML4 ML5

Causal Analysis and Resolution

Identify the causes of selected outcomes and take actions to prevent adverse outcomes from recurring and propagate positive outcomes.

TS Development ML3

Technical Solution

Select, design, and implement solutions to requirements, including evaluating alternative solution approaches and architecting the product.

PI Development ML3

Product Integration

Assemble product components and deliver the product by ensuring components are compatible, integrated in the correct sequence, and tested as an assembly.

VV Development ML3

Verification and Validation

Confirm that work products and the final product meet specified requirements (verification) and fulfill intended use (validation).

SD Services ML2 ML3

Service Delivery

Deliver services in accordance with service agreements, manage service delivery, and meet established service levels.

IRP Services ML2 ML3

Incident Resolution and Prevention

Identify, control, and address incidents and service requests in a timely manner to restore normal service and prevent recurrence.

CONT Services ML3

Continuity

Establish and maintain plans to ensure continuity of service delivery when significant disruptions occur.

SSD Services ML3

Service System Development

Analyze, design, develop, integrate, verify, and validate service systems that satisfy existing or anticipated service agreements.

SST Services ML3

Service System Transition

Deploy new or significantly changed service system components into the operational environment while maintaining service continuity.

STSM Services ML3

Strategic Service Management

Establish and maintain standard services in accordance with strategic needs and plans, and ensure services align with organizational goals.
