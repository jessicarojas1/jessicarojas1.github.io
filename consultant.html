What I Offer

📋

CMMC Readiness Assessments

Gap analysis against all 110 CMMC Level 2 practices. I map your current controls against NIST SP 800-171 and produce a prioritized remediation roadmap with realistic timelines.

📄

SSP & Policy Development

End-to-end System Security Plan authoring, policy templates, POA&M management, and evidence collection strategies designed to survive C3PAO assessment scrutiny.

DFARS / ITAR Compliance

Guidance on DFARS 252.204-7012 self-attestation, ITAR classification, CUI boundary definition, and flow-down requirements to subcontractors.

My Background

I've spent the past several years leading CMMC compliance programs for defense contractors as an Enterprise Systems Manager at GMRE, Inc. My work has included achieving CMMC Level 2 certification, authoring SSPs, performing NIST 800-171 assessments, building continuous monitoring programs, and guiding organizations through the full DoD authorization lifecycle. As a U.S. Army veteran I understand the mission context behind these requirements — this isn't just checkbox compliance, it's protecting critical national security information.

I hold active CISSP, CEH, ECES, and CompTIA Security+ certifications with a Master's degree in Cybersecurity and Information Assurance. I've also mentored cybersecurity professionals through Fullstack Academy, giving me a depth of experience in both hands-on technical work and translating complex compliance concepts into practical action plans.
