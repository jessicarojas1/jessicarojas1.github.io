AeroMarkup — Quick Guide

Mark up engineering drawings and photos of aircraft and parts — sketch, point, annotate, and measure. Works on 2-in-1s, iPads, Android tablets, and phones, with or without internet.

  • Pen / Highlighter — freehand sketch & scribble. Pressure-sensitive with a stylus.
  • Arrow — point to a feature. Drag from base to tip.
  • Note / Pin — tap to drop a text callout or marker.
  • Shapes — line, rectangle, ellipse for circling areas.
  • Pan / Pinch — two fingers to pan & zoom. Space-drag with a mouse.
  • Load Image — bring in a drawing or photo to mark up.
  • Sync — pushes your work to the server when online; everything is saved locally first, so it never blocks offline.

All work is saved automatically to this device. Offline edits sync the next time you press Sync while connected.
