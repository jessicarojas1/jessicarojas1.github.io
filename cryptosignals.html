📖 How to Read These Signals
🚀 STRONG BUY / BUY
RSI is oversold (below 30–35) and/or price has pulled back significantly. Asset may be undervalued short-term. Consider entering a position in small increments — don't go all in at once.
⏸ HOLD
No clear edge in either direction. RSI is neutral (40–60), momentum is mixed. Best to wait for a clearer signal before entering or exiting.
🚫 SELL / STRONG SELL
RSI is overbought (above 65–70) and/or recent price gains are large. Consider taking some profit or tightening your stop-loss. Not a guarantee of a drop.
📊 RSI Explained
RSI (Relative Strength Index) measures how fast price moved recently. Below 30 = may be oversold (beaten down too much). Above 70 = may be overbought (run up too fast).
