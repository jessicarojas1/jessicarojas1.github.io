Instructions: Complete all questions applicable to your organization. For "Yes" answers, be prepared to provide supporting evidence (policies, certifications, audit reports). For "Partial" answers, describe what is in place and what is outstanding. Responses are treated confidentially and used solely for risk assessment purposes. False or misleading responses may result in contract termination.

Section 1 — Information Security Governance

#QuestionYes / Partial / No / N/AEvidence / Comments
1.1Does your organization have a documented Information Security Policy approved by senior management and reviewed at least annually?
1.2Does your organization hold ISO 27001 certification or an equivalent (SOC 2 Type II, CSA STAR Level 2, FedRAMP)? If yes, provide certification details and scope.
1.3Is there a designated CISO or equivalent senior information security leadership role with defined responsibilities?
1.4Is information security performance reviewed by senior management at least annually, with documented outcomes?
1.5Does your organization conduct formal information security risk assessments at least annually?
1.6Are information security responsibilities included in all employee contracts and relevant job descriptions?
1.7Has your organization had an independent security assessment (penetration test or third-party audit) within the past 12 months?
1.8Does your organization maintain a comprehensive inventory of information assets?
1.9Has your organization experienced a confirmed security breach affecting customer data in the past 24 months? If yes, describe the incident and remediation actions taken.
1.10Does your organization have a documented information security improvement roadmap with defined objectives and timelines?
