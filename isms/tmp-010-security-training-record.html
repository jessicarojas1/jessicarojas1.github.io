Instructions

This record tracks security awareness training completion for all in-scope personnel. Update when: a new hire completes onboarding training (within 5 business days of hire); annual training is completed; phishing simulations are run (quarterly); AUP acknowledgment is re-signed.

Status Definitions: Current = All required training complete and not overdue. Overdue = Any required training past deadline. Exempt = Documented exemption (e.g., extended leave; retraining required on return).

Phishing Result Codes: P = Passed (did not click or reported) | C = Clicked link | CE = Clicked and entered credentials | R = Reported to security team (positive) | RT = Required remedial training after simulation.
