1. Instructions for Use

This Asset Inventory records all information assets within the ISMS scope as required by ISO 27001:2022 controls 5.9 (Inventory of information and other associated assets) and 5.10 (Acceptable use of information and other associated assets). Every asset that processes, stores, or transmits information relevant to the ISMS must be included. Assets must have a named owner who is responsible for their appropriate classification, protection, and lifecycle management.

This inventory must be reviewed and updated at least annually, when assets are added or decommissioned, and following significant changes to the IT environment. The asset list feeds directly into the risk assessment process (TMP-002) — all assets should be considered during risk identification.

Tip: When working with this document in a spreadsheet application, use filter/sort functions on the Asset Type, Classification, or Business Criticality columns to focus your review on specific asset categories.

Assets are organized into the following categories. Each asset record must be assigned one of these types:
