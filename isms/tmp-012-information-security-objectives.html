1. Purpose

This template supports compliance with ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Clause 6.2 (Information security objectives and planning to achieve them) and Clause 9.1 (Monitoring, measurement, analysis, and evaluation). It provides a structured format for setting, tracking, and reporting on measurable information security objectives that are consistent with the organisation's Information Security Policy and risk treatment priorities.

Objectives documented here must be communicated to relevant personnel, updated when circumstances change, and formally reviewed at each Management Review (see TMP-007).

2. ISO 27001:2022 Requirements — Clause 6.2

Each objective must satisfy all of the following mandatory criteria.

# Requirement Clause Evidence / Notes
1 Consistent with the information security policy 6.2(a) [Reference POL-001]
2 Measurable (where practicable) 6.2(b) Each objective has a KPI target and measurement method
3 Takes into account applicable IS requirements and risk treatment results 6.2(c) [Reference PRO-001 risk register]
4 Communicated to relevant personnel 6.2(d) Shared via [intranet / all-hands / email] on [date]
5 Updated as appropriate 6.2(e) Change log maintained in Section 7
6 Plans document: what will be done, resources needed, responsible party, completion date, evaluation method 6.2(f) Captured in objectives table (Section 4)
