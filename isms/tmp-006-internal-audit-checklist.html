Clause 4 — Context of the Organization

RefRequirementAudit QuestionEvidence RequestedFindingEvidence RefNotes
4.1Understanding the organization and its contextHas the organization identified internal and external issues relevant to ISMS purpose and direction?ISMS context document; management review records (TMP-007)
4.2Understanding needs of interested partiesAre interested parties and their information security requirements documented and monitored?Interested parties register; contractual requirements; regulatory mapping
4.3Determining scope of the ISMSIs the ISMS scope documented, approved by management, and does it reflect actual operations and interfaces?Scope document (TMP-011); management sign-off
4.4ISMSIs the ISMS established, implemented, maintained, and continually improved in line with ISO 27001:2022?ISMS documentation set; management review records; audit program

Clause 5 — Leadership

RefRequirementAudit QuestionEvidence RequestedFindingEvidence RefNotes
5.1Leadership and commitmentDoes top management demonstrate active support for the ISMS through resource allocation, communication, and participation?Management review minutes (TMP-007); IS policy signed by top management; budget records
5.2Information security policyIs an IS policy established, approved by top management, communicated to all personnel, and available to interested parties?POL-001; communication records; employee acknowledgments (TMP-010)
5.3Organizational roles, responsibilities, and authoritiesAre ISMS roles and responsibilities clearly assigned, documented, and communicated?Job descriptions; RACI matrices; organization chart; appointment letters

Clause 6 — Planning

RefRequirementAudit QuestionEvidence RequestedFindingEvidence RefNotes
6.1.1Actions to address risks and opportunitiesHas the organization determined risks and opportunities relevant to ISMS and planned actions to address them?Risk register (TMP-002); risk treatment plan; SoA (TMP-001)
6.1.2Information security risk assessmentIs a risk assessment methodology defined? Are assessments conducted at planned intervals and when significant changes occur?PRO-001; POL-017; completed risk assessments; TMP-002
6.1.3Information security risk treatmentIs a risk treatment plan documented with owner assignments, timelines, and residual risk evaluation? Is the SoA current and approved?PRO-002; TMP-001 (SoA); TMP-002; management approval records
6.2Information security objectivesAre IS objectives established, measurable, monitored, communicated, and updated as appropriate?TMP-012; progress reports; management review records
6.3Planning of changesAre changes to the ISMS planned and managed in a controlled manner?Change records; impact assessments; PRO-008

Clause 7 — Support

RefRequirementAudit QuestionEvidence RequestedFindingEvidence RefNotes
7.1ResourcesAre adequate human, financial, and technical resources allocated for the ISMS?Budget records; staffing plans; tool licenses and subscriptions
7.2CompetenceIs the competence of ISMS personnel defined, assessed, and maintained? Are records retained?Job descriptions; training records (TMP-010); certifications; competency assessments
7.3AwarenessAre personnel aware of the IS policy, their contribution to ISMS effectiveness, and the implications of non-conformance?Awareness training records (TMP-010); phishing simulation results; LMS completion reports
7.4CommunicationIs an IS communication plan defined? Is IS information communicated internally and externally as needed?Communication plan; distribution records; meeting minutes; newsletters
7.5Documented informationIs documented information controlled per PRO-003? Are documents created, updated, and distributed in a controlled manner?Document register; version history; approval records; PRO-003; obsolete document handling

Clause 8 — Operation

RefRequirementAudit QuestionEvidence RequestedFindingEvidence RefNotes

8.2Information security risk assessment (results)Are risk assessment results documented and retained? Are assessments current?TMP-002 with completion dates; risk assessment reports; approval records
8.3Information security risk treatment (results)Is the risk treatment plan implemented? Are results documented?TMP-002 residual risk columns; treatment evidence; implementation records

Clause 9 — Performance Evaluation

RefRequirementAudit QuestionEvidence RequestedFindingEvidence RefNotes
9.1Monitoring, measurement, analysis and evaluationAre IS performance metrics defined, monitored, analyzed, and reported? Are methods for monitoring defined?KPI dashboards; monitoring reports; TMP-012; management reporting
9.2Internal auditIs an annual internal audit program established? Are audits conducted by competent, independent auditors? Are results documented and acted upon?Audit program; previous audit reports; this checklist; auditor qualifications; CAR records
9.3Management reviewDoes top management review the ISMS at planned intervals with all required inputs? Are outputs documented and acted upon?TMP-007 records; attendance records; action logs; frequency meets requirements

RefRequirementAudit QuestionEvidence RequestedFindingEvidence RefNotes
