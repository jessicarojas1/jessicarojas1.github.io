1. Purpose

The Statement of Applicability (SoA) is a mandatory document required by ISO 27001:2022 clause 6.1.3. It records all controls from Annex A of ISO/IEC 27001:2022 and provides a determination of whether each control is applicable to the organization's ISMS. Where a control is included, the SoA documents the justification for inclusion and current implementation status. Where a control is excluded, the SoA documents the justification for exclusion and confirms that the associated risks have been accepted or treated through other means.

The SoA is a living document and must be reviewed and updated at least annually, following significant changes to the organization's risk environment, and as part of any ISMS management review. It serves as a key reference document for internal and external audits.

4. Control Applicability Determination

For each control below, complete all columns. Use the following guidance:

  • Applicable: Enter "Yes" if the control applies within your ISMS scope, "No" if it does not.
  • Justification: For "Yes" controls, reference the risk(s) or requirement(s) driving inclusion. For "No" controls, state the specific reason the risk does not exist or is addressed otherwise (e.g., "Organization does not develop software — no custom code exists").
  • Implementation Status: Select Planned, In Progress, Implemented, or Not Applicable.
  • Reference: Link to the policy, procedure, or evidence document that implements the control.
