1. Purpose

This document defines the scope of the Information Security Management System (ISMS) in accordance with ISO/IEC 27001:2022 clause 4.3. The scope establishes the boundaries and applicability of the ISMS, including which parts of the organization, which locations, which assets, and which activities are included. A clearly defined and approved scope is a mandatory prerequisite for ISMS certification and provides the foundation for all risk management, control selection, and audit activities.

The scope must consider: the external and internal issues identified in clause 4.1, the requirements of interested parties identified in clause 4.2, and the interfaces and dependencies between activities performed by the organization and those performed by other organizations. Exclusions from scope must be justified; the ISO standard does not permit the exclusion of controls simply because they are difficult to implement.
