Frequently Asked Questions

Who do you typically work with? +
Primarily small to mid-size DoD contractors (50–500 employees) who need to achieve CMMC Level 1 or Level 2 certification. I also work with subcontractors handling CUI who have flow-down DFARS requirements.
Do you work remotely? +
Yes — all assessments, policy work, and advisory services are delivered remotely. For engagements requiring on-site presence (e.g., physical environment walkthroughs), travel can be arranged.
How long does a CMMC Level 2 assessment take? +
A gap assessment typically takes 2–4 weeks depending on the size of the organization and availability of documentation. Full implementation programs range from 3 to 12 months based on the gap findings.
Can you help us after the C3PAO assessment? +
Absolutely. Post-assessment support — POA&M remediation, continuous monitoring, and annual review — is one of the most common reasons clients engage on retainer.
Do you sign NDAs? +
Yes. I routinely sign mutual NDAs before starting any engagement. I hold an active Secret clearance and understand the sensitivity of information involved in defense contractor environments.
