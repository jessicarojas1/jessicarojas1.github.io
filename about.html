My Story

I'm a cybersecurity professional with roots in the U.S. Army, where I served as Military Police with the Utah Army National Guard from 2015 to 2022. That experience gave me something you can't learn in a classroom: a mission-driven mindset, disciplined attention to detail, and an instinct for protecting critical assets under pressure.

After transitioning to civilian tech, I pursued a B.S. in Computer Science with a Minor in Military Science from Weber State University, then a Master's in Cybersecurity and Information Assurance from Western Governors University. Today, I serve as Enterprise Systems Manager at GMRE, Inc. — a defense contractor — where I led the end-to-end CMMC Level 2 certification effort, authored the SSP, and built the compliance infrastructure from the ground up.

I also mentor the next generation of cybersecurity professionals as a Cyber Mentor II at Fullstack Academy, translating complex concepts into practical, hands-on learning. Building this portfolio site — 20+ tools, a knowledge base, a compliance tracker, and a live AI agent — is part of the same philosophy: learn by building, share what you know.

How I Work

Mission before checkbox

Compliance isn't the goal — protecting the mission is. Every control I implement is grounded in why it matters, not just what the auditor wants to see.

Build, don't just advise

I write SSPs, author policies, tune SIEMs, and build tools. If something doesn't exist, I create it — from this portfolio to a full compliance agent powered by Claude.

Teach what you know

Mentoring at Fullstack Academy reinforced that the best way to deepen expertise is to explain it clearly to someone else. I bring that same instinct to client work.

Continuous improvement

Security is never done. I pursue certifications, build new tools, and stay current with evolving frameworks because the threat landscape never stops moving.
